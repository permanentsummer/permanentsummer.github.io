About Us

Get to know
us a bit more.

So… who’s behind this?

Family-run.
Algarve-born.

Permanent Summer is a small family-run brand born in Altura, in the eastern Algarve — where the summers are long, the light is golden, and the sea is always close.

Christopher is Mauritian by origin and Portuguese by heart. Two islands, two coastlines, one obsession with warm weather. That double dose of sunshine runs through everything we make.

How this all began?

From a garage.
To your wardrobe.

It started with frustration. Christopher kept looking for simple, well-made summer pieces — nothing loud, nothing cheap. He couldn’t find them. So he made them.

What began as a side project in the family garage in Altura quickly became something real. A brand built on one idea: that summer isn’t just a season. It’s a feeling worth wearing every day.

What do we actually make?

Designed with care.
Made to last summers.

Every Permanent Summer piece is built around one deliberate detail: our embroidered heart. It’s not a logo for show — it’s a mark. A quiet reminder that you chose something made with intention.

Heavyweight tees. Structured caps. Bucket hats that look better with every wear. Small runs. No shortcuts.

What’s our manifesto?

Summer is a feeling,
not a forecast.

Summer always comes back. That certainty — that warmth on the horizon — is what Permanent Summer is built on.

We believe in slowing down. In wearing something simple that makes you feel good. In the kind of day where you have nowhere to be and the sun has nowhere to go. Sun. Salt. Simplicity. Always.

Wear the feeling.

Organic cotton pieces made for every kind of summer day.
