About Us

Permanent Summer was born in the Algarve, where summer doesn't just arrive — it lingers. We believe summer is more than a season. It's a feeling: warm sand between your toes, salt on your skin, sunsets that seem to stretch forever.

Our brand was founded by Christopher, a Portugal-based creator who wanted to bottle that feeling into clothing that speaks without shouting. No oversized logos. No loud branding. Just thoughtful design with a minimalist heart — literally.

The Heart

Every Permanent Summer piece carries our signature embroidered heart. Small, precise, and intentional. It represents that one moment when the day is perfect, the light is golden, and everything just feels like summer.

What We Make

We design premium-weight t-shirts, structured caps, and bucket hats — all crafted from organic and sustainable cotton. Our pieces are made to be worn all day, every day, through every kind of summer adventure.

Where We Are

We're based in Altura, in the eastern Algarve, and we ship across Portugal and Europe. Every order is packed with care and sent directly from us to you.
