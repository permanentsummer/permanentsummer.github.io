Shipping Policy

We ship all orders from Altura, Portugal. All purchases are processed through Stripe and fulfilled within 1–2 business days of receiving payment confirmation.

Tracking

Once your order has shipped, you'll receive a confirmation email with a tracking number. You can use this to follow your package in real time.

Delays

During peak periods (summer, holidays, launches) shipping may take slightly longer. We'll always communicate any known delays via email.

If your order hasn't arrived within the expected window, please contact us at christopher@permanentsummer.co or via WhatsApp.

Customs & Duties

For deliveries within the EU, no customs duties apply. For orders outside the EU (when available), the recipient is responsible for any local customs fees or import taxes.
