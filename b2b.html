The Process

Simple from start to finish.

01

Submit request

Fill the form with product choice, quantities and upload your logo. We accept AI, EPS, SVG, PDF or high-res PNG.

Within 24h we send a personalised quote with pricing, lead time and a digital embroidery mockup.

03

04

Delivered

Packed and shipped directly to you. Portugal 2–4 days. Europe 5–10 days.
